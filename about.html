Your Trusted One-Stop Store

Welcome to Kaushar Investment (Pty) Ltd — your reliable one-stop departmental store proudly based in Gaborone, Botswana. We are dedicated to supplying quality products across a wide range of categories, all under one roof.

We believe every customer deserves the best value for their money. Our team is passionate about quality, service, and making sure you find exactly what you're looking for — whether in-store or through our fast delivery options across Gaborone and nationwide.
